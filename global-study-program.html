GLOBAL STUDY PROGRAM

EARN A DEGREE FROM A LEADING INTERNATIONAL UNIVERSITY AT A SUBSTANTIALLY REDUCED COST

STUDY IN INDIA AT AMITY FOR ONE OR TWO YEARS.

THEN, TRANSFER TO AMITY'S PARTNER UNIVERSITY IN USA, UK, CANADA OR AUSTRALIA.

WHY GLOBAL STUDY PROGRAM

GSP is a unique international lateral transfer program, where the students spend one or two years at Amity in India and the balance number of years of the degree at a University of their choice abroad.

The final award of degree is from the Foreign University at a fraction of the cost compared to studying for the entire duration abroad.

GLOBALLY-RECOGNISED DEGREE

Earn a degree from a leading university of USA, UK, Canada or Australia

ECONOMICAL

Save on international fees and living expenses, while you complete part program in India at Amity

DIVERSE COURSES

Choose from over 100 UG and PG courses across 15 universities in the USA

CREDIT TRANSFER OPPORTUNITIES

All your study credits at Amity are 100% transferable to the partner university in USA

STAY BEYOND STUDY

Provision for easy Post Study Work Permit

EARN WHILE YOU LEARN

Upto 20 hrs. per week of study work permit to every student

BRICS Internship Programs

BRICS Educational Internship Program was held at Far Eastern Federal University, Vladivostok, Russia, a strategic partner of Amity from Russia. The Program was held as a semester educational internship organized by Far Eastern Federal University (FEFU) during the spring semester (mid-February - June 2020). , It was designed to provide an environment for students from Brazil, Russia, India, China and South Africa to study together in the framework of educational programs that could be evaluated as the most popular and relevant for BRICS.

20 Brilliant students from Amity were shortlisted from India, out of which 18 from different Amity campuses participated from the field of Engineering, Management, Health Sciences and International Relations. This was 100% funded by the BRICS committee, including Tuition, Accommodation, Airfare fees etc.

This gave true international hand on experience to the students as it involved industry projects, research exploration, thesis preparation and also learning the basic Russian language. Further it promoted academic and cultural exchange aimed at intensification of socio-humanitarian cooperation, strengthening dialogue and mutual understanding within BRICS member states.
