Transforming the Education Landscape

Over the last 30 years our not-for-profit Amity Foundation has established top-ranked universities and schools across the world which are at the forefront of cutting-edge innovation and teaching methodologies. With 175,000 students across 1,000 acres of campuses and 15 million sq ft of buildings we have a deep commitment to transforming and nurturing future leaders.

Present across
the world

Our campuses across the world in 12 countries give great exposure to our students and faculty and enable us to benchmark with the best in the world.

Partnering with the best

We have partnerships with some of the leading scientists and Universities across the world in areas of collaborative research, curriculum development, faculty & student exchange, as well as global study and twinning programs.

Top Global Rankings

The unwavering commitment towards imparting world-class education is the hallmark of Amity Education Group. In fact, its flagship university has been ranked among the top 3% universities globally by QS & THE, the world's leading university rankings organisations.

It has also set a new standard of academic excellence by becoming Asia’s only not-for-profit university to be awarded US Regional Accreditation and the UK’s Quality Assurance Agency for Higher Education.

Amity University Greater Noida

Amity University in Mohali (Punjab) is the tenth university to be established by the not-for-profit Amity Foundation. Being set up as a multi-disciplinary university, it offers UG, PG and Doctoral Programmes in over 25 disciplines. Located on a 40 acre campus just 10 minutes away from the Chandigarh International Airport, it is proposed to be the hub of hi-end research, with extensive collaborations with top global universities to facilitate research opportunities for faculty and international exposure for its students.
