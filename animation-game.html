Course Overview

BDes (Animation & Game Designing), a 3-year programme, focuses on the conceptualisation, coding and designing of animated games. The course imparts in-depth knowledge about the fundamentals of game development that includes strategy, creativity and implementation. Students of the programme learn the basics of game and animation programmes, 3-D modelling and animation, storytelling, storyboarding and graphic design skills among others.

The programme instils in students advanced technical skills and ability to code languages. They also learn expert usage of a variety of tools and processes like level and map editors, databases, proprietary scripting languages and design documents.

Career Options

The gaming industry is growing at an incredible pace and is expected to reach a whopping $180.1 billion by 2021 in the US alone. Today games have found extensive usage in industries as diverse as events & marketing, banking, hotel, and education among others. In this context, the importance of game designing as an extremely promising career is a certainty.
